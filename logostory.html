Our Logo.



Why have a bee as your logo when you are in the business of Pest Control you ask?



Well, we have a healthy respect for nature, the environment and all the critters in it. All insects and animals have their rightful place, and only become a pest to us when they encroach on our space. Otherwise we believe, they deserve to be left alone.

Bees are industrious, you’ve heard the expression ‘A busy little bee’. Or when your sporting club wants everyone to pitch in and get work done they call it a ‘Busy Bee’.

Bees are essential to us humans. Without bees we would cease to be. They go about their work in a busy, industrious, co-operative way, and contribute to this world of ours being a better place.



So, Why not a bee?



He embodies all of the characteristics we admire, and represents the values we hold dear.

We would encourage you to please learn more about beautiful bees.
